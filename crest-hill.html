Signs You Need Drain Cleaning

Don't wait until it's a full backup. These are signs your drains need attention now:

  • 🐌 Water draining slowly in sinks, tubs, or showers
  • 💨 Gurgling sounds coming from drains
  • 🤢 Foul odors from drains or near toilets
  • 🚽 Toilet backing up or overflowing
  • 💧 Multiple drains clogged at the same time
  • 🌊 Water pooling in your yard (possible sewer line issue)

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between snaking and hydro jetting?

Snaking breaks up clogs mechanically. Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to fully clear buildup from pipe walls — better for grease, roots, and recurring clogs.

How often should I have my drains cleaned?

For most homes, once a year is enough to prevent buildup. If you have recurring clogs, more frequent cleaning or a camera inspection may be needed.

Can you clear a sewer line backup?

Yes — sewer line backups are one of our specialties. We use camera inspection to locate the blockage and hydro jetting to clear it completely.
